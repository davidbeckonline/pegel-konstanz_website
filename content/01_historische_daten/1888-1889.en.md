## Historical Water Levels of Lake Constance

## 1889

The level of Lake Constance (station Konstanz) in year 1889.

- Maximum: 461 cm (06/08)
- Minimum: 256 cm (01/30 until 01/31)

## 1888

- Maximum: 519 cm (08/07)
- Minimum: 254 cm (03/09)
